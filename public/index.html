Non-Verbal Communication

Who should use this app?

Anyone who has suffered some kind of brain injury and has difficulty communicating, also caregivers and family members who what to communicate with them

About this app

This is a very simple site for those who have difficulty finding words and communicating their wants and needs to their families and caregivers

What does it do?

This app allows you to create cards with images along with a text description so that you can communicate better without the need to search through long lists of words to find what you want to say

How does it work?

You will be shown step by step how to create a category, subcategory and item along with an selecting a matching image to display on the card
